## Why This Matters for the PM

When a PM reviews content, they must ask:
1. **Would MY child want to solve this?** (playfulness)
2. **Does it guide thinking or just test memory?** (thought direction)
3. **Is the help helpful or just giving answers?** (guidance quality)
4. **Does it make them curious for more?** (curiosity building)

Each dimension answers these questions differently. The PM needs to see ALL dimensions to understand the RANGE of what we can build.
